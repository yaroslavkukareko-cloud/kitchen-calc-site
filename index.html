Features

Designed to stay simple, useful, and pleasant to use.

Measure converter

Convert common cooking measurements for ingredients such as flour, sugar, water, oil, milk, butter, rice, oats, and salt.

Proportion calculator

Quickly solve recipe-style proportions in a clear fraction layout by filling any three fields and calculating the missing value.

Useful kitchen units

Includes metric-style values and common US kitchen units like cups, tablespoons, teaspoons, and liquid-based measures.

Made for fast everyday use

The popup remembers entered values during the browser session, so you can reopen it without losing your current calculation.
